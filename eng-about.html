I have always believed that my destiny was to make people feel better.

For this reason I graduated in the Social-Health sector, but my true inclination emerged when I discovered Traditional Chinese Medicine. TCM is simple, immediate, a bit like old grandma's remedies. A completely different approach to what we are used to, the person is seen in his holistic aspect and not as a leg or back to be examined and treated.

At this point I started studying again, a lot of theory and countless hours of practice!

I graduated from the “Il Giardino di Hoshin” Association and thanks to it I am in possession of the professional certification FISTQ - Italian Federation of Tuina and Qigong Schools.

My professionalism is verified and certified annually by the OTTO Professional Association.

I am constantly updating to continue improving the techniques I know and learn new ones to always offer superior quality services.

For years I have been practicing Taijiquan, Qigong and meditation to increase and preserve my sensitivity and my energy balance.



If I managed to make my dream of becoming a TuiNa Operator come true, I owe it to the special people who have directed and helped me all these years.

Thanks to Walter, Claudio, Paolo and Lucia, Ines, Marisa and Fiorella.
